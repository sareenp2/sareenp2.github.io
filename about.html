About me

Sareen Podhuturi

Systems Engineer

My name is Sareen Podhuturi and I am a recent graduate of the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign with a degree in Systems Engineering and Design with a Secondary Field in Business Systems Integration and Consulting. During my four years at the University of Illinois I was able to take advantage of a variety of extracurricular activities that have allowed me to gather substantial experience in a diverse range of real-world scenarios.

While working to achieve my degree I was given the opportunity to become a senior consultant with Illinois Business Consulting (IBC), which is the largest student run management consulting organization in the nation. As a senior consultant I was given valuable exposure to real-world issues that allowed me to develop both my leadership and analytical skills. During my time with IBC I learned not only how to effectively communicate with fellow team members but also how to clearly layout timelines and project objectives to ensure the team functioned most effectively.

In addition to my time as a senior consultant with IBC I was given the opportunity to work for Magna International as a corporate R&D intern. This experience allowed me to gain firsthand knowledge about how modern engineering research and development functions as well as learn about the unique process of evolving from prototype to production specification.

In the complex modern world that we find ourselves in businesses are rapidly changing and are being forced to reevaluate their approach to every aspect of their operation. My rather unique combination of technical skills, proven leadership abilities and diverse work experience are what enable to make a valuable addition to any team.
